Cheese Celebration Details

Overview

This romantic and intimate cheese celebration is designed for two, featuring curated cheese options from local shops in Heidelberg, Melbourne, Victoria.

Shop Suggestions

  • Leo's Heidelberg: Known for gourmet cheese options, including vegetarian-friendly varieties.
  • The Vegan Dairy: Offers handmade cheese alternatives perfect for unique pairings.
  • Food to Make You Smile: Wide range of artisanal cheeses, including options like feta and ricotta.

Item List

  • Soft Cheese: Brie or Camembert (~200g)
  • Firm Cheese: Cheddar (~250g)
  • Specialty Cheese: Goat cheese (~150g)
  • Crackers, Fruits, Nuts, and Honey for pairing

Pairing Ideas

Enhance your platter with:

  • White wine: Perfect for soft cheeses
  • Red wine: Complements firm cheeses
  • Sparkling water or mocktails for non-alcoholic options
