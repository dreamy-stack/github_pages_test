Welcome to Your Cheese Celebration!

Celebrate in style with curated cheese options and tips!

Where to Buy Cheese

  • Leo's Heidelberg - Gourmet cheese options. Address: 133–137 Burgundy Street, Heidelberg.
  • George Eats Recommendations - Vegetarian cheese guide. Link
  • Maxi Foods Supermarket - Specialty cheeses. Location: Ferntree Plaza, Upper Ferntree Gully.

Cheese Selection and Quantities

Perfect quantities for two:

  • Soft Cheese: Brie or Camembert (~200g, Milawa Cheese Company)
  • Firm Cheese: Mungalli Creek Cheddar (~250g)
  • Specialty Cheese: Goat cheese from Meredith Dairy (~150g tub)
  • Blue Cheese: Small wedge (~100g)

Pairings

Enhance the cheese platter with:

  • Artisanal crackers or sourdough bread (~150–200g)
  • Fresh fruits (grapes, figs, pears) (~300g mixed)
  • Nuts: Walnuts or roasted almonds (~50g)
  • Honey or chutney (~100g jar)

Preparation Tips

  • Let cheese reach room temperature for flavor.
  • Pair with white wine (Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc) for soft cheeses or red wine (Shiraz) for firm cheeses.
  • Arrange on rustic platters with accents.
  • Light candles for ambiance.
